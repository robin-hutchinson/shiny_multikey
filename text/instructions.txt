Ladybird pupa species is included as Phalacrotophora do have a host preference, however they can parasitise other ladybird species and so this question should not be used in isolation.

Male specimens of P. delageae and P. berolinensis cannot be reliably separated - record these as an aggregate unless a female is also collected. P. harveyi has only been collected as a female, therefore they cannot yet be identified using male genitalia.
